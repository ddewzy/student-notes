How to make a peanut butter sandwich

  1. Gather bread, peanut butter, preferred jelly and a butter knife
  2. Lay out bread slices and begin spreading peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Put slices together and enjoy!!
